2. Description of Service

HUD Live Captioning is a mobile application that provides real-time speech-to-text transcription functionality by:

  • Converting spoken audio to text using Apple's on-device Speech framework
  • Displaying live captions in real-time on your device
  • Providing a windshield HUD display mode with mirror-reversed text
  • Using OpenDyslexic font for improved readability
  • Saving conversation history locally and syncing to Firebase Firestore
  • Syncing settings and statistics across devices via Firebase
  • Requiring authentication (Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In, or Email/Password)

All speech recognition and transcription happens on your device using Apple's Speech framework. We do not collect, store, or transmit your audio or transcription data to our servers.

Your conversation history, settings, and statistics are stored in Firebase Firestore and synced across your devices. This data is private to your account and encrypted in transit. We cannot access your conversation content.

When you sign out, all local data is cleared from your device. When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted from Firebase.
