Introduction

HUD Live Captioning ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. By using HUD Live Captioning, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of your conversation data.

HUD Live Captioning is designed with privacy as the top priority. All speech recognition and transcription happens entirely on your device using Apple's on-device Speech framework. Your conversations never leave your device.

The app may collect:

  • Device Information: Basic device information for crash reporting and analytics (if you opt-in)
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous usage data to improve app performance (if you opt-in)
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information
  • No Conversation Data: Transcriptions are stored locally on your device only. We never see or access your conversations

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect your conversation data, we cannot use it. Any anonymous usage data collected (with your permission) is used solely to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Understand which features are most useful

Data Storage

All transcriptions and conversation data are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync (optional), your conversation history may be synced to your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is encrypted and only accessible to you through your Apple ID.

We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs how iCloud data is handled.

Third-Party Services

HUD Live Captioning uses Apple's Speech framework for on-device speech recognition. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used in this app.

Data Security

Your data security is important to us. Since all transcription happens on-device and we don't collect your conversation data, your information is as secure as your device. We recommend:

  • Using a strong device passcode
  • Enabling Face ID or Touch ID
  • Keeping your iOS software up to date

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all conversation data from your device at any time through the app settings
  • Disable iCloud sync if you don't want your data backed up
  • Export your conversation data as text files
  • Use the app completely offline without any data transmission

Children's Privacy

HUD Live Captioning is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
