Getting Started

  1. Grant Permissions: Allow microphone and speech recognition access when prompted.
  2. Start a Call: Begin your phone call or meeting.
  3. Tap "Listen": Start transcription with one tap.
  4. Read Along: Watch live captions appear in real-time.
  5. Save: Conversations are automatically saved when you stop.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use in a quiet environment for best accuracy
  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Position your phone to capture audio clearly
  • Adjust brightness for optimal visibility while driving
  • Use mirror mode when reflecting on windshield

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes! All transcription happens on your device using Apple's Speech framework. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your conversation data. Your privacy is our top priority.

Does it work offline?

Yes! The app uses on-device speech recognition, so it works without an internet connection once the language model is downloaded.

Can I export my conversations?

Yes! You can export saved conversations as text files from the conversation history in Settings.

Why are there duplicates in my transcript?

The app uses smart deduplication to keep only the longest, most complete version of each sentence. If you see duplicates, please update to the latest version.

How do I use HUD mode while driving?

Mount your phone on your dashboard, enable mirror mode, and the text will reflect on your windshield. Adjust brightness for optimal visibility.
